About
Four years.
Many hats.
No half measures.

I started in content — learning how words shape perception, how a single line can make someone stop scrolling. That foundation never left me. It just got bigger. Over four years I moved from writing to strategy, from strategy to creative direction, from creative direction to leading a team that does all of it.

At Novel Office, I built the brand's voice, tone and guidelines from the ground up — and then made sure they showed up consistently across every channel, every campaign and every piece of collateral we touched. I don't just run campaigns. I build the systems behind them.

EmailAutomation
Building a Lifecycle Marketing System
Designed and implemented automated email nurture workflows — onboarding, re-engagement and retention flows with proper segmentation and A/B testing.

Built a structured lead management system that handles every enquiry — from the moment someone submits a website form or writes directly via email — through to full team notification and follow-up. Every step is defined, every person knows their role, and no lead falls through.

On contact, a thank-you email is automatically triggered to the lead. Internally, the enquiry flows through a defined pipeline: admin filter → ERP entry → marketing handoff → engagement summary → full team briefing.

📧 Automated lifecycle flows across onboarding & retention
