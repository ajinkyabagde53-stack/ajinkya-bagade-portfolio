About
Four years.
Many hats.
No half measures.

I started in content — learning how words shape perception, how a single line can make someone stop scrolling. That foundation never left me. It just got bigger. Over four years I moved from writing to strategy, from strategy to performance, from performance to leading a team that does all of it.

At Novel Office, I built the brand's voice, tone and guidelines from the ground up — and then made sure they showed up consistently across every channel we touched. I don't just run campaigns. I build the systems behind them.

01
BrandContent Strategy
Building Novel Office's Brand Foundation
Developed the brand's voice, tone and guidelines from scratch — creating a cohesive identity that could scale across every channel and every team member.

When I joined Novel Office, the brand didn't have a defined identity. Every piece of communication sounded different. I built the brand guidelines from the ground up — documenting the voice, tone, messaging pillars and visual direction that now govern everything we produce.

This wasn't just a document. It became the operating system for the entire marketing team. Every brief, every post, every email now starts from a clear foundation.

📋 Brand guidelines now used across all channels
Brand StrategyFigmaCopywriting
02
Social MediaContent
Revamping Social Media from the Ground Up
The brand's social channels were inconsistent — no rhythm, no voice, no structure. I rebuilt the entire approach and the results followed.

I took a hard look at how the brand was showing up online and decided it wasn't working. Rebuilt the content approach from scratch — new formats, a defined brand voice and a creative pipeline that kept the team producing without burning out.

Built a weekly content calendar, introduced content briefs for every post and established a review process that kept quality consistent across the board.

📈 11K+ reach — highest since launch in 2024

PerformancePaid Media
End-to-End Paid Campaign Management
Managed Google Ads and Meta Ads across the full funnel — from audience segmentation and creative direction to budget management and performance reporting.

Worked closely with media buyers to ensure paid and organic strategies complemented each other. Introduced structured reporting templates that replaced manual data pulls and gave the team weekly visibility into campaign health.

Audited existing ad accounts, identified inefficiencies and restructured campaigns to improve targeting and reduce wasted spend.

🎯 Improved campaign structure and reporting efficiency

04
EmailAutomation
Building a Lifecycle Marketing System
Designed and implemented automated email nurture workflows — covering onboarding, re-engagement and retention flows with proper segmentation and A/B testing.

Cleaned the database, set up proper audience segmentation and built responsive templates that matched the brand's visual identity. Ran ongoing A/B tests on subject lines, layouts and CTAs.

The result was a system that ran itself — consistently delivering the right message to the right person at the right time.

📧 Automated lifecycle flows across onboarding & retention
MailChimpSegmentationA/B TestingCanva
05
AIAutomation
Automating Monthly Marketing Reports
Monthly reporting was a manual, time-consuming process. I built an automated system that consolidated performance data across channels into a clean, shareable format every month.

Used AI tools to build a workflow that pulls data, formats it and produces a consistent monthly report — removing the dependency on any one person and freeing the team to focus on actual marketing work.

⚡ Reporting time cut significantly month-on-month
