About

Building a Lifecycle Marketing System
Designed and implemented automated email nurture workflows — onboarding, re-engagement and retention flows with proper segmentation and A/B testing.

Built a structured lead management system that handles every enquiry — from the moment someone submits a website form or writes directly via email — through to full team notification and follow-up. Every step is defined, every person knows their role, and no lead falls through.

On contact, a thank-you email is automatically triggered to the lead. Internally, the enquiry flows through a defined pipeline: admin filter → ERP entry → marketing handoff → engagement summary → full team briefing.

Novel Office · Meta Ads
Performance Ad Creatives

Oversaw the paid social strategy for Novel Office across Meta. Campaigns structured across three funnel objectives — building awareness with cold audiences, re-engaging warm traffic, and converting intent with direct offers.

Brand Awareness
Top-of-funnel creatives served to cold audiences. Goal: introduce Novel Office as a premium managed workspace and drive initial brand recall.
Retargeting
Mid-funnel creatives served to warm audiences — people who'd visited the site, watched a video, or interacted with previous ads. Goal: bring them back and reinforce the proposition.
Lead Generation
Bottom-of-funnel creative with a direct incentive. Goal: convert high-intent prospects into enquiries and trial bookings with a time-sensitive offer.
